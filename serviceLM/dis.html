Clearing and Settlement

Efficient and Secure DIS Clearing Services

At Trishakti Securities, we manage your Delivery Instruction Slips (DIS) with the highest level of care and attention to detail. Our DIS clearing services ensure that your stock transactions are cleared accurately and efficiently, minimizing the risk of errors and ensuring smooth settlement of your trades. Whether you're transferring securities or completing a delivery of shares, we are committed to reducing delays and providing you with a seamless trading experience.

What is DIS Clearing?

Delivery Instruction Slip (DIS) is a critical document used by investors to authorize the transfer of shares from their Demat account to another. It is used primarily for the delivery of securities in both market and off-market transactions. The DIS process helps ensure that securities are transferred securely and that all regulatory requirements are met during the clearing and settlement process.

Why DIS Clearing is Important

DIS clearing is an integral part of the settlement process in securities trading. It ensures that when you sell or transfer shares, they are delivered to the buyer or recipient without any issues. Proper handling of DIS ensures compliance with SEBI regulations, reduces the chances of errors, and guarantees that transactions are completed on time. Our team handles the entire process to ensure that your trades are cleared accurately and efficiently.

Key Features of Our DIS Clearing Services

  • Accurate Processing: Every DIS is carefully verified and processed, ensuring that the right shares are transferred and that there are no discrepancies in the transaction.
  • Timely Settlement: Our systems ensure that your shares are delivered on time, meeting all deadlines and ensuring smooth settlement of trades.
  • Compliance with Regulations: We strictly adhere to the guidelines set by regulatory bodies like SEBI, ensuring that your transactions comply with all industry standards.
  • Minimizing Errors: Our team works diligently to reduce the risk of errors in the DIS process, preventing issues like mismatched securities or incomplete transfers.
  • Secure Transfers: We prioritize the security of your shares during the DIS process, ensuring that all transfers are carried out in a safe and compliant manner.

How the DIS Clearing Process Works

Our efficient process for handling DIS requests involves several key steps to ensure the accurate and timely transfer of your securities:

  1. Submit Your DIS: Fill out the Delivery Instruction Slip with the necessary details, such as the beneficiary’s Demat account number, ISIN, and the number of shares to be transferred.
  2. Verification: Our team verifies the DIS and cross-checks the details to ensure accuracy and compliance with regulatory guidelines.
  3. Processing: Once verified, we initiate the transfer of the securities from your Demat account to the designated recipient’s account.
  4. Settlement Confirmation: After the transfer is completed, both parties (you and the recipient) receive a confirmation of the successful transaction.
